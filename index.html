During this time, I learnt on the job, either from others or many a late-night, researching solutions required for the particular task at hand. Job Title

Head of IT / CIO / CTO

Job Description

My ideal job is the Head of Technology or (Chief Technology Officer) for a progressive company that includes the use of Information Technology in their strategic plan. Another variant of this role is the Chief Information Officer (CIO), the difference being that a CTO is typically in charge of the technology being used externally for a busines such as a product, whereas the CIO oversees how a company uses and protects its information internally. Often in small to medium businesses one role covers both these responsibilities. Shown on the left is a snippet from a CTO role advertised on Seek. I believe that being directly involved with the direction of a business at an executive level, would be highly rewarding, not only from a remuneration perspective but also a sense of self achievement

Skills Required

Typically, a CTO requires many years of experience in the industry, understand technology trends, and have proven management skills in order to lead a team to success. An understanding of business practices and budgeting is also required in order to be able to make informed decisions, balancing technology requirements to financial resources. Project management and people skills are also required in order to ensure that required business outcomes are achieved, and staff remain engaged and rewarded for their efforts. As with any leadership role, excellent communications skills and professional business manner are a must.

Skills I have

Having worked in a Management role in the Industry for some time, I believe I possess a number of these skill required. In fact, in my most recent role I was considered to be in a Head of IT position in a small business as part of the Executive Team reporting to the Managing Director.

Although I have no actual qualifications ‘on paper’ I have experience evolving technology and working with team members to progress projects. I have strong communication skills, am very good at problem solving, including an analytical mind that can separate the actual facts from rumour and have experience ensuring that tasks and projects align with a business’s strategic direction.
